"Portgas D. Ace"is a prominent character in One Piece and Luffy's adoptive brother. He was the captain of the
Spade Pirates and later served as the second division commander of the Whitebeard
Pirates. Ace gained the ability to control fire after eating the Mera Mera no Mi Devil Fruit. Known for his
loyalty and strong bonds with his crew, Ace is a beloved figure whose story plays a significant role
in the series.
