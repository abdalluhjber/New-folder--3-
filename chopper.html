Tony Tony Chopper is the doctor of the Straw Hat Pirates in One Piece. He is a reindeer who ate the Hito Hito
no Mi, a Devil Fruit that allows him to transform into a human-reindeer hybrid. Chopper is known
for his adorable appearance and often misunderstood behavior, as people tend to see him as a pet
rather than a capable doctor. Despite his cute demeanor, he is a skilled medical professional and a
fierce fighter, especially when using his various transformation forms. His dream is to become a doctor
who can cure any disease.
