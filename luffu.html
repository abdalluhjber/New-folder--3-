Monkey D. Luffy
is the main protagonist of One Piece and the captain of the Straw Hat Pirates. He possesses the ability to stretch his body like rubber after eating the Gum-Gum Fruit (Gomu Gomu no Mi). Luffy dreams of becoming the Pirate King by finding the legendary One Piece treasure and gaining the title of the strongest. Known for his carefree personality, bravery, and strong sense of justice, Luffy inspires those around him and is fiercely loyal to his friends and crew.
