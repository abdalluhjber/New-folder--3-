Roronoa Zoro
is the swordsman of the Straw Hat Pirates in One Piece. He is known for his exceptional swordsmanship, using a unique three-sword style called Santoryu. Zoro dreams of becoming the world's greatest swordsman to honor his childhood friend, Kuina. With his strong sense of loyalty and unwavering determination, Zoro is a key member of the crew and often acts as a protector during their adventures.
